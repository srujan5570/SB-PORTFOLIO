Summer Superfoods

🥭 Best Foods for Summer

  • Watermelon, cucumber, mint for cooling hydration
  • Mango, orange, muskmelon — vitamin-rich cooling fruits
  • Curd, buttermilk, moong dal for light digestion
  • Nuts, seeds, banana, chia for sustained energy

🚫 Avoid in Summer

  • Spicy and fried foods that raise internal heat
  • Sodas and soft drinks — dehydrating and harmful
  • Excess caffeine — worsens dehydration
  • Heavy non-vegetarian meals during peak heat hours
